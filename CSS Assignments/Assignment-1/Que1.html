🎬 Behind-the-Scenes & Trivia

  1. 🗓 Fast Production
    2. The entire film was shot in just over two months, which is considered quick for a full-length Telugu feature film.
  2. 🎤 Vijay Deverakonda’s Singing Debut
    3. The song "What The Life" was sung by Vijay Deverakonda himself, marking his singing debut in Telugu cinema.
  3. 🎧 Music Went Viral Before Release
    4. The track "Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale" became a viral hit even before the movie was released, gaining millions of views within days.
  4. 🤝 Real Chemistry On-Screen
    5. The lead actors Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna received praise for their natural chemistry, which was partly due to their friendly off-screen rapport.
  5. 💰 Low Budget, Big Success
    6. The movie was made on a modest budget (~₹5 crore) and earned over ₹130 crore, making it one of the biggest blockbusters of 2018 in Telugu cinema.
  6. 🎥 Filming Locations
    7. Key scenes were shot in Hyderabad, Vizag, and Araku Valley, showcasing beautiful visuals without needing foreign locations.
  7. 🛑 Censor Troubles (Minor)
    8. The film received a U/A certificate from the Censor Board with a few minor suggested cuts, mainly in comedic scenes.
